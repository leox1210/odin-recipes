Atol Chuco

The Atol shuco , or just shuco , is a typical hot drink of Guatemala , El Salvador and some border towns of Honduras . It is composed of fermented corn dough, water, salt, and alguashte.

Ingredients

  • roasted corn
  • purple corn
  • red beans
  • a guacal
  • morro
  • salt
  • aguashte
  • lemon
  • chile

Steps

  1. Black corn is roasted in a griddle or saucepan
  2. The roasted corn is placed in a container with water and left to rest overnight.
  3. The next day the fermented black corn is ground.
  4. Add water to this mass and strain it into a straining blanket , the liquid is drained.
  5. This liquid is cooked in a pot, stirring constantly until it boils.
  6. When ready, a small portion of the atol is mixed separately with alguashte.
  7. It is served in a huacal de morro, placing first the atol, then black beans salted with broth and salt, followed by alguashte. To this you can add chili to taste.
